How to Upload Your Data

You will use a pre-formatted CSV template to import data for the list of Suppliers you procure your items from. Once imported you will be presented with the results in the management grid below and have the opportunity to configure optional settings that will improve your forecasts and purchase recommendations. Itâ€™s helpful to import your suppliers so you can associate them with your items. ForecastRx will suggest Purchase Orders based on those associations!

  1. Download the template from the “Bulk Managment” dropdown in the management grid below.
  2. Open the templete in a CSV editor (i.e. Microsoft Excel)
  3. Add one row for each Supplier, making sure not to modify any of the column headers.

Required

  • Supplier Name - The common business name of your suppliers.

Recommended (for improved forecasts and purchase calculations)

  • Lead Time - The number of days it takes for the item to become saleable, including any assembly/manufacturing, from the time you place the order.
  • Order Interval - The preferred number of days between placing purchase orders.
  • FBA Replenishment Model - Show how you typically replenish products so your forecast will account for the correct lead times when calculating your restock suggestions. (Possible values: GLOBAL, LOCAL, DIRECT_SHIP)

Example (Apply for the default template)
