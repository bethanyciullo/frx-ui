Ideal FBA Inventory

Choose a range for the number of days of inventory coverage you'd like to keep at Amazon.
Products quantities below your minimum will be flagged orange in your Restock Suggestions.
Products quantities between your minimum and maximum will be flagged yellow in your Restock Suggestions.
